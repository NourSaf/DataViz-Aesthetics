Research Reports

📁 Shared Google Drive Folder Links to an external site.

 

Description

Your Research Report should cover the following

  • History & Origins
    Make a research presentation that thoroughly investigates the origins and contemporary usage of your selected chart type.
  • Data Types
    Explain what type of data your visualization is useful for (i.e. numeric, times series, aggregate, qualitative, relationships, etc)
  • Formal Design Elements
    Your presentation should detail the formal elements (e.g. line, color, shape, scale) that are utilized by your chart type.
  • Contemporary Examples
    Include at least three contemporary applications and prominent projects that highlight advantages or disadvantages to your chart type.
  • Presentation
    You will present your research report in front of the class on your assigned due date detailed in Canvas. Your presentation should take around 10–15 minutes.
  • Class Discussion
    The last slide of your presentation should include a visualization example that we use to facilitate class discussion.

Learning Objectives

By completing this assignment, you will be able to:

  • Describe the origins and history of a chart
  • Describe the formal structure and design elements of a chart in detail
  • Describe the ideal applications of a chart and misuses of a chart

Recommended Reading

Heer, Jeffrey et al. “A Tour through the Visualization Zoo.” https://queue.acm.org/detail.cfm?id=1805128 Links to an external site. 

Submitting Assignment

Your research report will be submitted online using Canvas.

Your research report should be a powerpoint (or equivalent) presentation exported as a PDF file. 

Your presentation will also be added to a shared Google folder that is shared with the class.
